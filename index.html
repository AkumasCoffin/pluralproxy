About

Built for plural systems.

Plural Proxy is a private platform designed to help DID, OSDD, and other plural systems organize and understand themselves better.

Every alter gets a rich profile with dozens of customizable fields — from basic info like name and pronouns, to detailed notes on triggers, boundaries, coping strategies, and therapy notes.

The relationship map gives you a visual overview of how alters relate to each other and to external people. The friends system lets you selectively share alters with trusted people, so they can better understand your system.

Your data is private by default — nothing is shared unless you explicitly choose to share it, and everything is securely stored.
